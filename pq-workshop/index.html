Workshop on the representation theory of p-adic and quantum groups and connections to geometry and combinatorics

University of Amsterdam / Korteweg-de Vries Institute for Mathematics

General Information: Recently, many interesting connections have been discovered between the representation theories of quantum groups and p-adic groups (some examples include, but are not limited, to the work of Gurevich on the Gan-Gross-Prasad conjecture, the work of Buciumas-Patnaik on the metaplectic Casselman Shalika formula and quantum groups at a root of unity, or the work of Miemietz-Stroppel on quiver Schur algebras and p-adic groups). Some of these ideas relate to problems in geometric Langlands (Gaitsgory-Lysenko), as well as combinatorially to the work of Sahi-Stokman-Venkateswaran. The purpose of this workshop is to gather specialists in these areas and allow them to exchange ideas.

Organizer: Valentin Buciumas. Contact at valentin dot buciumas at gmail dot com.

Time and Place: The workshop will take place May 1-3 2023 at the University of Amsterdam.
