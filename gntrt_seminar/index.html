Geometry, Number Theory, and Representation Theory Seminar

An online seminar hosted at the University of Alberta

General Information: The Geometry, Number Theory, and Representation Theory seminar at the University of Alberta meets once a week on Tuesday for one hour research talks followed by a discussion session.

Organizers: Valentin Buciumas, Mathieu Dutour and Manish Patnaik.

Announcements: The zoom link and password will be sent by email to registered participants one day before the talk. If you're interested in attending, please register here.

Time and Date: The date and time are always Mountain Standard Time. The seminar usually meets on Tuesdays at 10am, but there will be exceptions to accommodate speakers from different time zones.
