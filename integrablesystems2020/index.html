New Connections in Integrable Systems

September 29 – October 2, 2020

General Information: This four-day workshop over Zoom will feature talks highlighting the applications of integrability across a broad range of disciplines like mathematical physics, combinatorics, representation theory, algebraic geometry and number theory. The conference will bring together participants from a wide range of backgrounds in order to share their work, exchange ideas and possibly forge new collaborations.

Organizers: Valentin Buciumas and Ole Warnaar. If you have any questions, please email Valentin at v.buciumas@uq.edu.au.

Time Zone: Australian Eastern Standard Time (AEST). Note that 9am AEST = 8am Tokyo time = 7pm EST the day before.

Registration: The zoom link and password will be sent by email to registered participants before the conference. If you're interested in attending, please register here.

Discussion sessions: The discussion sessions will take place over gather.town. The link and password for the gather.town rooms will be sent before the conference begins.
