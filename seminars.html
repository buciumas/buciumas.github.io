Conferences and Seminars

I am currently organizing the POSTECH Representation Theory Seminar.
I used to organize the POSTECH-PMI Number Theory Seminar.

I have previosly (co-)organized the following conferences and seminars:

  • In February 2025 I co-organizing a lecture series on the relative Langlands in February 2025.
  • In May 2023, I organized a workshop on the representation theory of $p$-adic groups and connections to quantum groups, geometry and combinatorics.
  • Co-organizer of the conference From $E_6$ to $\widetilde{\widetilde{E}}_{60}$ in honor of Eric Opdam at University of Amsterdam; August 31 - September 2, 2022
  • Misha Ishachenkov and Jasper Stokman and I organized the WAYTA research seminar at University of Amsterdam during 2021-2022.
  • Mathieu Dutour and Manish Patnaik and I organized a research seminar on geometry, number theory and representation theory at University of Alberta in 2020-2021.
  • With Ole Warnaar, I organized a conference on integrable systems in September 2020.
  • Masoud Kamgarpour, Anna Puskas and I organized the WiSe learning seminar at University of Queensland in 2020.
  • Co-organizer with Ting Xue for the Special Session on Representation Theory at the 2019 Australian Mathematics Society Meeting, December 3-6, 2019, Melbourne, Australia
  • I organized a learning seminar on integrable lattice models at University of Queensland in 2019.
