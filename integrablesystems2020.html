NEW CONNECTIONS IN INTEGRABLE SYSTEMS

SEPTEMBER 29 - OCTOBER 2, 2020

General Information: This four-day workshop over Zoom will feature talks highlighting the applications of integrability across a broad range of disciplines like mathematical physics, combinatorics, representation theory, algebraic geometry and number theory. The conference will bring together participants from a wide range of backgrounds in order to share their work, exchange ideas and possibly forge new collaborations.

Organizers: Valentin Buciumas and Ole Warnaar
